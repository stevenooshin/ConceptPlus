THE IDEAS

Four ways to tell one story.

Directions, not final films — each a different way to show who Concept Plus is, what it does, and where it's going. The loops are look references only.

01
The Concept Film

It starts with a concept.

A cinematic presentation of the holding itself — who Concept Plus is, what it does, and the vision behind it. From a blank page to a group with seven engines: development, real estate, marketing, media, hospitality, art, academy. Each one earns its moment on screen, all of it orchestrated from one core. Projects like Oystra appear as proof of the system — not the subject. Two to three minutes, narrated, graded matte and filmic. Never plastic, never "AI-made."

02
The Door

A door to the vision.

One door standing in the void, then in nature — no walls, pure concept. Each time it opens, a different world Concept Plus builds: development, hospitality, art, the academy. Abstract, timeless, made to loop for years without ageing. CGI-grade — fully AI, or finished in 3D.

03
Sketch to World

One line becomes a home.

The most visual of the four — no story to follow, just transformation you can't look away from. A single line mutates: into soft-body 3D form, into structure, into a lived space. Material shifts, light shifts, scale shifts. Bold Apple-grade type lands each service on the beat. Pure motion craft — orchestration, made visible.

Look reference
04
The House & The Home

From imagined, to lived.

The feeling of House of CP, on film. We start close — materials, texture, finish: stone, bronze, warm light, the details that separate a real space from a rendered one. Then the finished project. Then the people, and the room comes alive. As the camera travels, each service switches on a zone — development raises it, design dresses it, hospitality warms it, the academy trains the person who greets you. This one isn't about explaining. It's about how the place makes you feel.Runs as one cinematic — or splits into a venue film + a living film.
