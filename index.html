The House & The Home

From imagined, to lived.

mediaidea-04-house-and-home.mp4

The feeling of House of CP, on film — and the life it leads to.

We start close. Materials, texture, finish: stone, bronze, warm light — the details that separate a real space from a rendered one. Then the finished project. Then the people, and the room comes alive.

As the camera travels, each service switches on a zone — development raises it, design dresses it, hospitality warms it, the academy trains the person who greets you. Oystra appears as proof of the system, not as the subject.

This one isn't about explaining. It's about how the place makes you feel.

One film. Two masters.

Built for two different rooms — the screens that run all day, and the meetings where decisions get made.

A

The Venue Cut

2 minutes · silent

Built for the House of CP screens. No voice-over — the screens play muted all day, so the film carries itself on image, rhythm and type. Long enough to live behind a room without repeating itself.

B

The Story Cut

1 minute · voice-over + music

The sharp version. Voice-over and music, cut tight. For presentations, socials, investor meetings, and anywhere the story has to land in sixty seconds.
